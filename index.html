Journey with Cancer

On August 16, 2023, Desirae went into the ER for chest pains and severe bruising. Doctors found fluid surrounding her right lung and chest and on August 23rd, 2023, she was diagnosed with Stage 2 Primary mediastinal B-Cell Lymphoma (PMBL). The bright gold section is the tumor in the middle of her chest.
